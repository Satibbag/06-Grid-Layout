Tickets

Ticket Types

  • Single-Day Ticket - Enjoy a full day of magic with a one-day ticket to Disneyland or Disney California Adventure. Starting at $104.
  • Multi-Day Ticket - Maximize your visit with a multi-day ticket, allowing you to experience more attractions and shows. Prices vary based on the number of days.
  • Annual Pass (Magic Key) - Unlock a year of Disney magic with the Magic Key pass, offering park reservations, discounts on food and merchandise, and special perks.

Add-Ons

  • Genie+ Service - Skip the line at select attractions with the Genie+ service. Includes unlimited Disney PhotoPass downloads for the day.
  • Park Hopper - Access both Disneyland and Disney California Adventure in one day with a Park Hopper upgrade.

Tips & Guides

Tips for First-Time Visitors

  • Arrive early to make the most of your day and enjoy shorter wait times.
  • Download the Disneyland app for maps, wait times, and mobile food ordering.
  • Bring a refillable water bottles to stay hydrated throughout the day.

Best Times to Visit

  • Weekdays during the school year are generally less crowded.
  • Mid-January to mid-March offers cooler weather and lighter crowds.

FAQs
